<Section icon={Swords} title="Competitive Rivals vs. Persistent Voyage">

The choice between Rivals and Voyage represents the core split in the **sand raiders of sophie game modes**. The "Rivals" setting is the standard extraction experience where you are pitted against other Raiders who are just as hungry for resources as you are. The "Strong Rival" setting specifically increases the difficulty and the potential quality of loot dropped by opponents and environmental hazards.

In contrast, **Voyage** is built for the long haul. It removes the pressure of a ticking clock, allowing you to explore the submerged islands of Sophie at your own pace. This mode is particularly useful for players who want to focus on the "Raiders of Sophie" lore, visiting the remnants of the Austro-Hungarian luxury without being forced into a firefight every five minutes.

| Feature | Rivals (Strong) | Voyage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PvP Intensity** | Very High | Moderate/Low |
| **Loot Quality** | **Exotic/Legendary** | Common/Rare |
| **Match End** | Extraction/Death | Manual Leave |
| **Resource Density** | High | Distributed |
| **Risk Factor** | ★★★★★ | ★★☆☆☆ |

<Callout type="warning" title="High Stakes">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Change Game Modes: Step-by-Step">

Switching between the different **sand raiders of sophie game modes** is a straightforward process, but it must be done from the main lobby or the overview screen before a match begins. You cannot change these settings once you have deployed to the planet's surface.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Overview">
    Navigate to the main game menu and ensure you are in the **Overview** section. This is where your character and current Trampler are displayed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Open Mode Selection">
    Click on the **Select Mode** option, usually located near the "Play" or "Deploy" button.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Toggle Rivals or Voyage">
    Choose between the available modes. If you want a challenge, select **Strong Rival**. For a more relaxed session, pick **Voyage**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Select Crew Size">
    After picking the mode, select your crew configuration: **Solo**, **Small Crew**, or **Large Crew**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Confirm and Launch">
    Verify your loadout and click the launch button to begin your descent to planet Sophie.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Matchmaking Lock">
Once you select a mode and begin matchmaking, your settings are locked. If you wish to change from Solo to a Crew, you must cancel the queue and restart the process.
</Callout>

</Section>
