<Section icon={Shield} title="The Trampler: Engineering Your Survival">

In this **SAND Raiders of Sophie review**, we must emphasize that your Trampler is not just a vehicle; it is your character's lifeblood. Unlike other shooters where you lose a backpack, losing a Trampler in *SAND* is a catastrophic logistical failure. The editor allows you to place engines, reactors, storage modules, and crew rooms with total freedom.

The architecture of your walker directly impacts your survival strategy. A chaotic interior might confuse boarding parties, while a streamlined layout allows your crew to reach the steering wheel or repair stations faster during a crisis.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Weaponry and Parts: 2026 Tier List">

Combat in *SAND* is split between mounted heavy weaponry and handheld player gear. Our **SAND Raiders of Sophie review** finds that while the 80mm Mounted Cannon is the king of the dunes, utility items often dictate who actually makes it to extraction. <Section icon={Map} title="Game Modes: Voyage vs. Storm Dive">

A major part of any **SAND Raiders of Sophie review** is deciding where to spend your time. The game offers two distinct flavors of extraction. **Voyage** is a persistent open-world mode where you set your own pace. It is the ideal environment for testing new Trampler blueprints and learning the map's procedural layout without constant pressure.

**Storm Dive**, however, is the "Battle Royale" of extraction shooters. A sandstorm slowly closes in on the center of the map, forcing players into a violent confrontation. The rewards are significantly higher, but the risk of losing your entire walker is constant.

| Mode | Risk | Rewards | Timer | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Voyage** | Low | Steady | None | New players, Resource farming |
| **Storm Dive** | High | Massive | Storm-based | PvP crews, High-tier loot |

<Callout type="info" title="Matchmaking Note">
Matchmaking is based on crew size. Solos are capped at 15 players per server, while large crews (4-6) can face up to 45 players total.
</Callout>
