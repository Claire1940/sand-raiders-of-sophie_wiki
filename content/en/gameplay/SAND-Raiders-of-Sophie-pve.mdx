<Section icon={Target} title="Looting Routes and High-Value Locations">

Exploring Sophie requires a tactical approach to movement. Since PVE players want to maximize loot while minimizing risk, understanding the procedural layout of cities, ruins, and shipwrecks is vital. Every expedition should have a planned route that includes multiple Points of Interest (POIs) and a clear path to an extraction point.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Scan the Horizon">
    Use your Trampler’s elevated position to spot smoke, ruined skylines, or shipwrecks. These indicate high-density loot areas.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Secure the Perimeter">
    Before leaving your Trampler to scavenge on foot, use mounted weapons to clear any immediate PVE threats surrounding the building or wreck.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Artifacts">
    Focus on high-value artifacts and rare weapon crates. In the 2026 meta, these provide the best return on investment for your time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor Cargo Levels">
    Once your storage compartments are 80% full, begin moving toward the nearest extraction point. Greed is the leading cause of lost Tramplers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Location Danger Levels">
- **Ruins**: Low risk, steady resources.
- **Shipwrecks**: Medium risk, high salvage potential.
- **Cities**: High risk, best weapon and artifact drops.
- **Dreadnought**: Extreme risk, only for prepared crews.
</Callout>

</Section>
