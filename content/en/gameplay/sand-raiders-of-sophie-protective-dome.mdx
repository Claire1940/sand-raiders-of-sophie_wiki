<Section icon={Shield} title="Understanding the sand raiders of sophie protective dome">

In the harsh, dieselpunk deserts of Sophie, survival depends on how well you can protect your crew and your Trampler walking mech. The **sand raiders of sophie protective dome**—specifically known in-game as the **Domovyk Protective Dome**—serves as the ultimate mobile defense shield. When deployed, it projects a massive blue energy field that absorbs all incoming weapon fire and cannon shells, keeping your team safe during intense extraction battles.

Unlike standard cover, this deployable dome offers 360-degree protection, allowing your squad to regroup, revive fallen allies, or repair critical Trampler systems under heavy siege. In a game where losing your ship means losing your hard-earned progress, mastering the placement of these defensive barriers is the difference between a successful extraction and a devastating defeat.

There are four distinct deployable dome technologies available in the game, each serving a unique tactical purpose during a raid. Understanding how they compare is essential for building a balanced squad loadout.

| Dome Name | Color | Primary Effect | Tactical Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Domovyk Protective Dome** | Blue | Absorbs all incoming damage | ★★★★★ |
| **Pestkop-Lorenz Amplifier** | Yellow | Boosts firearm and cannon damage by +100% | ★★★★★ |
| **Podorozhnyk Bio-Emitter** | Green | Generates a dome that heals players over time | ★★★★☆ |
| **Von Liebig Reflector** | Metallic | Projects a directional shield reflecting damage | ★★★★☆ |

<Section icon={Map} title="Farming Red Boxes for Defensive Deployables">

To secure the coveted Domovyk Protective Dome, you must hunt down Red Boxes. Red Boxes represent the highest tier of loot containers on Sophie, far outclassing the common White and Green Crates. While standard crates contain basic crafting materials and low-tier weapons, Red Boxes house experimental technology, high-impact cannons, and game-changing deployables.

These rare containers do not spawn in standard shipwrecks or low-level areas. Instead, you must brave high-danger zones and named monuments to locate them. Because loot drops are randomized, you may need to open multiple Red Boxes before securing the specific protective dome you need.

| Container Type | Rarity | Primary Locations | Key Loot Drops |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **White Crate** | Common | Scattered ruins, basic shipwrecks | Crafting materials, standard ammo |
| **Green Crate** | Uncommon | Named monuments, outer building areas | Mid-tier weapons, basic Trampler parts |
| **Red Box** | Rare | Deep city centers, locked interior rooms | Experimental cannons, defensive domes |

<Section icon={Target} title="Tactical Deployment and Trampler Integration">

Once you have successfully extracted a protective dome, you must integrate it into your active setup. Deployables cannot be used directly from your global warehouse; they must be slotted into your loadout before starting a round. 

On board your customizable walking mech, deployables are stored on physical item shelves. During a firefight, a crew member must physically grab the device from the shelf and throw it to activate the dome. This physical interaction adds a layer of realism and urgency to combat, requiring clear communication among squad members.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Loot and Extract">
    Locate a Red Box in an urban monument, secure the Domovyk Protective Dome, and safely extract it back to your lobby inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip to Active Loadout">
    Before launching a new raid, navigate to your character inventory and drag the dome deployable into your active loadout slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Retrieve from the Trampler Shelf">
    Once the match begins, walk over to the item shelf on your Trampler walking mech to find your packed deployable dome.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deploy in Battle">
    Equip the device from the shelf and throw it onto the deck of your Trampler or onto the ground to activate the protective field.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Synergizing Domes for Ultimate Squad Defense">

While a single Domovyk Protective Dome offers excellent protection, combining multiple deployables creates an impenetrable fortress. Stacking different colored domes allows your squad to simultaneously heal, absorb damage, and double your offensive output.

For example, placing a Domovyk Dome (blue) alongside a Pestkop-Lorenz Amplifier (yellow) allows your team to stand inside a protected zone while firing outward with a massive +100% damage bonus. This synergy is particularly devastating when using high-powered vehicle weapons like the Experimental 80mm Naval Cannon.

| Combo Name | Dome Combination | Tactical Synergy | Combat Role |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Bastion** | Domovyk + Podorozhnyk | Provides total damage absorption while healing allies | Defensive Turtle |
| **The Firepower** | Domovyk + Pestkop-Lorenz | Protects shooters while doubling outgoing damage | Offensive Siege |
| **The Aegis** | Von Liebig + Pestkop-Lorenz | Reflects frontal attacks and boosts flank counters | Tactical Counter |
