<Section icon={Map} title="The Tech Tree: Unlocking the Arsenal">

Progressing your character is not just about leveling up; it is about navigating the complex technological remnants of the Empire. To access better **sand raiders of sophie weapons**, you must interact with the navigation bar in the upper left of your interface. The Tech Tree is divided into several branches, each representing different aspects of survival and industrial capability.

For weaponry, your focus must remain on the **LandV's Branch**, which is visually distinguished by its green color. This branch represents the engineering and combat advancements necessary to survive the post-apocalyptic landscape of Sophie.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Tech Tree">
    Open the main menu and navigate to the upper left navigation bar. Select the Tech Tree icon to view the available research paths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate LandV's Branch">
    Identify the green-colored tree. This specific path is dedicated to armament and Trampler combat enhancements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invest in Gun Upgrades">
    Look for the specific node featuring a gun icon. This is the gateway to expanding the Item Shop's inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Research Requirements">
    Ensure you have 1,500 Crowns and at least 200 Scrap Ammo. Once confirmed, initiate the research to unlock new purchasing options.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Requirement Type | First Unlock Cost | Source / Acquisition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Currency** | **1,500 Crowns** | Completing expeditions and selling loot |
