<Section icon={Target} title="Getting Started: Your First Extraction">

Once you have secured your copy, the transition from the orbital station to the desert planet Sophie can be brutal. The game does not hold your hand, and losing your first Trampler can be a significant setback. The core loop involves scavenging for resources, surviving encounters with other raiders (PvP) and local threats (PvE), and successfully extracting to upgrade your gear.

New players should prioritize learning the map layout in the lower-stakes "Voyage" mode before diving into the "Storm Dive" competitive mode. In Voyage, you can focus on resource gathering and understanding how your Trampler handles different terrains without the immediate pressure of a closing sandstorm.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Trampler Editor">
    Before deploying, use the editor to build your walking base. Ensure you have a balanced layout with an engine, reactor, and at least one storage compartment. Save this as a **blueprint** so you can rebuild quickly if destroyed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your Mode">
    Select **Voyage** for a persistent world experience or **Storm Dive** for a faster, battle-royale-style match with higher loot density. For your first five runs, Voyage is highly recommended.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scavenge and Survive">
    Land in a relatively quiet zone. Loot abandoned cities and shipwrecks for weapons and artifacts. Keep your Trampler within sight; it is your only ticket home and your primary mobile storage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Extraction Point">
    Locate an extraction zone early. As your cargo fills up, the risk of being intercepted by other crews increases. Head to the extraction point, defend the area until your transport arrives, and secure your loot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Solo Strategy">
If playing solo, build a compact, stealth-focused Trampler. Smaller vehicles are harder to spot on the horizon and can navigate tight city ruins more effectively than the massive fortresses used by larger crews.
</Callout>

</Section>
