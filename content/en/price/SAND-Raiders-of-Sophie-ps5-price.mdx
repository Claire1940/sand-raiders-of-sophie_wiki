<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Starting Your First Expedition">

Once you have navigated the **SAND Raiders of Sophie ps5 price** and secured your copy, your first hour in the desert will determine your long-term success. The game does not hold your hand, and losing your initial resources can be a setback.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Region">
    Inventory and tech tree progression are tied to your specific region. Choose the server closest to you to minimize latency during high-stakes PvP fights. Note that while Trampler blueprints are shared, your physical items are not.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Trampler Editor">
    Before heading out, use the Editor to customize your walking base. Ensure you have essential compartments: an **Engine**, a **Reactor**, and **Storage**. Without storage, you cannot bring loot back to the orbital station.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose Voyage Mode">
    For your first few runs, avoid **Storm Dive**. Choose **Voyage Mode** to learn the map layout, practice Trampler piloting, and scavenge low-tier ruins without the pressure of a closing sandstorm.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Loot and Extract">
    Scavenge cities and shipwrecks. Once your storage is 50-70% full, locate an extraction point. Greed is the leading cause of death on Sophie; it is better to leave with a small profit than die with a full cargo hold.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Solo vs. Crew">
If you are playing solo, build a compact Trampler. Large, multi-story fortresses are powerful but nearly impossible for a single player to defend against a coordinated boarding party.
</Callout>
