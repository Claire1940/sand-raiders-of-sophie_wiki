<Section icon={Swords} title="Trampler Engineering and Combat">

The heart of the game is the Trampler. On the **SAND Raiders of Sophie release day**, players have access to a deep modular editor. Unlike other base-building games, every module you add to your Trampler affects its weight, center of gravity, and power consumption. 

Building a "mechanical furniture" house will lead to a quick death; you must engineer a viable war machine.

| Module Type | Primary Function | Key Stat |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Reactors** | Provides power to all modules | Energy Output |
| **Engines** | Controls walking speed and torque | Horsepower |
| **Storage** | Increases loot capacity | Slot Count |
| **Armor Plates** | Protects internal compartments | Damage Reduction |
| **Turrets** | Automated or manual defense | DPS / Range |
