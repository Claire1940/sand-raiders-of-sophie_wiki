<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Tactics and Survival Strategy">

Combat in the **sand raiders of sophie play test** is split between long-range artillery duels and close-quarters boarding actions. When two Tramplers engage, the battle often becomes a race to see who can destroy the other's internal systems first.

**Key Combat Rules:**
- **Aim for the Legs**: Damaging a Trampler's chassis can slow it down or immobilize it.
- **Internal Sabotage**: If you board an enemy ship, focus on destroying their reactor or their beds to prevent them from respawning.
- **Repair Duty**: At least one crew member should always have a multi-tool equipped to fix pipes and walls during a fight.
