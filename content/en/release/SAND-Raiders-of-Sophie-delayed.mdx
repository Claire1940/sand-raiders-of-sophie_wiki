<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Guide to the Extraction Loop">

Entering the world of Sophie for the first time can be overwhelming. The core loop revolves around the **Trampler**, your mobile fortress. Unlike traditional extraction shooters where you only worry about your character's gear, in SAND, you must manage your vehicle's fuel, storage, and structural integrity.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Mode">
    Decide between **Voyage** and **Storm Dive**. New players should start in Voyage to learn basic Trampler handling and scavenging without the pressure of a closing sandstorm.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Scavenging Phase">
    Navigate your Trampler to cities, ruins, or shipwrecks. Use your on-foot character to enter buildings and find high-value loot, weapons, and resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defend Your Base">
    Rival players or AI threats may attack your Trampler while you are away. Ensure you have basic **Armor Plates** and **Mounted Turrets** to deter boarders.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Extraction">
    Locate an extraction point on the map. You must successfully navigate your Trampler to this zone and initiate the extraction process to secure your loot in the orbital station.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Starter Strategy">
In your first few runs, focus on gathering **Repair Kits** and **Storage Compartments**. Keeping your Trampler alive is more important than finding rare weapons. If you lose your Trampler and have no resources to rebuild, you may need to join a friend's crew or start a new character.
</Callout>
