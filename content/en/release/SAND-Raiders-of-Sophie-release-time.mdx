<Section icon={Target} title="Game Modes: Voyage vs. Storm Dive">

Understanding the different expedition styles is crucial once the **SAND Raiders of Sophie release time** is behind us and you are ready to deploy. The game currently offers two distinct ways to experience the planet Sophie, catering to different risk appetites and progression goals.

**Voyage Mode** serves as a persistent open-world experience. It is the ideal environment for new players to learn the ropes of Trampler navigation and resource management without the overwhelming pressure of a closing circle. In this mode, players decide their own extraction timing, making it perfect for steady scavenging and building up your initial resource pool.

**Storm Dive** is the high-stakes, action-oriented mode. Similar to battle royale mechanics, a sandstorm slowly engulfs the map, forcing crews toward the center. This mode features significantly higher-tier loot and artifacts but increases the likelihood of player encounters. Greed in Storm Dive is often the leading cause of Trampler loss.

| Feature | Voyage Mode | Storm Dive |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Risk Level** | Lower | Very High |
| **Loot Quality** | Standard | High-Tier / Rare |
| **Timer** | None (Persistent) | Sandstorm Shrinking |
| **PvP Focus** | Opportunistic | Frequent / Forced |
| **Best For** | Learning & Farming | High-Value Rewards |

<Callout type="tip" title="Solo Survival">

</Callout>

Once you have confirmed the **SAND Raiders of Sophie release time** and completed your download, follow these steps to secure your first successful extraction.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Trampler Editor">
    Before deploying, enter the editor to prepare your walking base. You can build from scratch or use blueprints. Ensure you have essential modules: an Engine for movement, a Reactor for power, and Storage for your loot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your Crew">
    Decide if you are going solo or with friends. Matchmaking brackets are based on crew size. Small teams (2-3) and large teams (4-6) have different server caps to ensure fair engagement distances.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scavenge Cities and Ruins">
    Deploy to Sophie and head toward ruined cities or shipwrecks. Your Trampler is your mobile fortress; don't stray too far from it on foot, as rival players or PvE threats can board and hijack your vessel.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Your Cargo">
    As you find weapons, ammo, and artifacts, store them in your Trampler's compartments. Remember: if your Trampler is destroyed and you don't have a blueprint or resources for a new one, you'll have to join another crew to restart.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Extract Safely">
    Locate an extraction point. In Voyage, you can leave whenever you feel your cargo is valuable enough. In Storm Dive, you must extract before the sandstorm reaches the center. Successful extraction secures your loot and your Trampler for the next run.
  </Step>
</Steps>
