<Section icon={Target} title="Getting Started: Your First Expedition">

Entering the world of Sophie for the first time can be overwhelming. The game's primary loop involves scavenging ruined cities, managing your Trampler's fuel and integrity, and extracting before rival players or the environment claim your life. Follow these steps to ensure your first run isn't your last.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Configure Your Trampler">
    Before deploying, use the **Trampler Editor** to set up your mobile base. Focus on **Storage Compartments** and **Repair Kits** for your first run. You can save your design as a **Blueprint** so you can rebuild quickly if your fortress is destroyed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Mode">
    Choose **Voyage Mode** if you are a solo player or a new crew. This mode offers a persistent open-world experience with lower stakes. For high-tier loot and intense PvP, enter **Storm Dive**, which features a shrinking sandstorm mechanic.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scavenge Cities and Wrecks">
    Navigate your Trampler toward POIs (Points of Interest) like ruined cities or shipwrecks. Leave your Trampler in a defensible position and head out on foot to gather resources, weapons, and **Artifacts**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure the Extraction">
    Once your storage is full, locate an extraction point. In Voyage, you can extract at your leisure. In Storm Dive, you must extract before the sandstorm reaches the map center. Successful extraction saves your loot, your character, and your Trampler.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Matchmaking Brackets">
SAND uses crew-size-based matchmaking to keep fights fair. Solos play on servers capped at 15 players, while large crews (4-6 players) play on servers with up to 45 players.
</Callout>

</Section>
