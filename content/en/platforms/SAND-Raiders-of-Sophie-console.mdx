### The Extraction Loop

Regardless of the mode, the core loop remains:
1.  **Scavenge**: Explore ruined cities, shipwrecks, and abandoned streets.
2.  **Loot**: Fill your Trampler's storage compartments with resources and artifacts.
3.  **Survive**: Defend against PvE threats (undead and automated systems) and rival players.
4.  **Extract**: Reach an extraction point and return to the orbital station with your haul.
