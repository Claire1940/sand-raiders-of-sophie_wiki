<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Gameplay: Tramplers and Extraction">

The heart of **SAND Raiders of Sophie ps5** is the Trampler. These aren't just vehicles; they are your mobile base, storage unit, and primary weapon system. On the planet Sophie, being caught without your Trampler is usually a death sentence. The game follows a high-stakes extraction loop where you must venture out, gather resources, and return to safety.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Trampler Editor">
    Before deploying, use the deep editor to install engines, reactors, and storage compartments. You can save these designs as **Blueprints**, which is vital if your walker is destroyed during an expedition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Scavenging Sophie">
    Navigate through ruined cities and shipwrecks. Use your Trampler to store heavy loot and provide a spawn point for your crew. Look for high-value artifacts but watch the horizon for rival players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Combat and Defense">
    Engage in ship-to-ship combat using mounted 80mm cannons or board enemy Tramplers for close-quarters fighting. Use the environment to hide your heat signature or set up ambushes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Successful Extraction">
    Reach the extraction point before your resources are depleted or your Trampler is disabled. Successfully extracting secures your loot and allows for permanent upgrades to your tech tree.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="Game Modes: Voyage vs. Storm Dive">

When you launch **SAND Raiders of Sophie ps5**, you will have the choice between two primary ways to explore the world. Understanding the risk-to-reward ratio of each mode is essential for long-term progression.

| Feature | Voyage Mode | Storm Dive Mode |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Risk Level** | **Lower** | **High** |
| **Loot Quality** | Standard / Common | Rare / High-Tier |
| **Timer** | No fixed timer | Limited by Sandstorm |
| **PvP Frequency** | Moderate | **High** |
| **Objective** | Scavenging & Learning | Battle Royale Style Extraction |

**Voyage Mode** is the persistent open-world experience. It is ideal for new players to learn how to steer their Tramplers, understand the map POIs (Points of Interest), and gather basic resources without the constant pressure of a closing storm.

**Storm Dive Mode** is for confident crews. A massive sandstorm slowly engulfs the map, forcing all players toward the center. This mode features the best loot but guarantees combat encounters as players are squeezed into smaller territories.

<Callout type="info" title="Matchmaking Info">
The game uses crew-size brackets for matchmaking. Solos are generally grouped with other solos (up to 15 per server), while large crews of 4-6 players are placed in high-capacity servers (up to 45 players or more).
</Callout>
