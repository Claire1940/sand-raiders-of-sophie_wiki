<Section icon={PlayCircle} title="Getting Started: First Session Setup">

Transitioning onto the planet Sophie requires more than just a purchase. You must prepare your mechanical base and understand the operational rules of the various **SAND Raiders of Sophie platforms**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Official Acquisition">
    Purchase the game via the [Steam Store](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1431300/SAND_Raiders_of_Sophie/) or add it to your console wishlist. Avoid third-party "key resellers" as they often lack legitimate launch rewards like the Server Slam skins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Trampler Editor">
    Before your first deployment, enter the Trampler Editor. Build a basic layout with an engine, reactor, and storage. Save this as a blueprint; if your Trampler is destroyed on Sophie, the blueprint allows you to reconstruct it quickly at the orbital station.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mode Selection">
    Choose between **Voyage** and **Storm Dive**. Voyage is a persistent, lower-stakes exploration mode ideal for learning the map. Storm Dive is a high-risk, high-reward mode where a sandstorm forces players toward the center, creating intense PvP bottlenecks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Extraction Loop">
    Enter the map, loot city ruins or shipwrecks, and store items in your Trampler's internal compartments. To keep your loot, you must navigate to a designated extraction point and successfully leave the planet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Trampler Loss">
If you lose your Trampler and have no resources to build a new one, you must either join a crew that owns a Trampler or create a new character. Managing your resources at the orbital station is just as important as surviving on the planet.
</Callout>

</Section>
